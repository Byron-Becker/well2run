Team Training

Train with a group of motivated, supportive runners of all ages and experience levels who share a true passion for running. Whether you want to train for your first race or you're a seasoned racer, there is a place for you in our team training programs.

Run Coaching

Whether you are just feeling flat in your training or ready to train for your first big race, I’m here to help you achieve your personal running goals. Let’s step up your running game by developing a custom training plan so that you can feel your absolute best while running.

Physical Therapy

If you suffer from aches and pains after every run, you’re not alone. Let’s get to the root of the problem and fix it forever. You deserve to be pain free and get back to doing what you love, running. I specialize in treating running injuries and can help you overcome yours.

Services

Run Gait Analysis

I perform in-person and online running gait analysis. Gait analysis can be a powerful way to determine if you have any unfavorable habits that are causing extra strain on your body. It can also help identify if you have any patterns that correlate with common injuries in runners.

Marathon Training

I offer a marathon training program that combines app-based run monitoring with personalized coaching. By analyzing your recorded runs, I tailor a training regimen to meet your unique needs. With my ongoing guidance, you'll optimize your performance and reach your marathon goals.

Post Partum Return to Run

I assist women in making a safe and successful return to running post-baby, whether you're 6 weeks or 6 years into motherhood. Understanding that each individual has a unique running history and birth experience, I offer specialized guidance as both a physical therapist and running coach with expertise in women's health.
